Explore Holy Trinity



We all desire to connect with others.

We all want to live deeply meaningful lives.

We all hope to make a difference.

We live in a fast-paced, rapidly changing world. This makes life exciting. But it also makes it hard to connect with others around the things that matter most. It makes it difficult to know who we are supposed to be with every passing trend and fad. And it makes it difficult to know how to make a difference where there are genuine needs.

We aspire to plant a church that helps us to put down deep roots in the ancient church so that we can live fuller, more meaningful lives today in our exciting times. We aspire to plant a church:

Where Christian Community connects us to one another around the things that matter most as we forge spiritual friendships and as we worship God in the beauty of Anglican worship.

Where Deep Discipleship takes place as together we find our true identity in Christ and as we take on spiritual disciplines that will sustain us as Christ’s disciples in a rapidly changing world.

Where Serving our City is prioritized—where we are more concerned with making a difference in our community than being served ourselves.

Fr. Sean cares deeply about this vision because the Church shared this great vision with him while he was spiritually wandering in his teens after his mother passed away. Sean wants to make that same difference for others.
