Ministries

As men and women commit to our launch team and bring their own gifts, talents, and passions, we envision that we will deepen our ministry and outreach to our community.

At present, we are currently partnering with the following ministries to meet the needs of those in our community:

True Freedom Ministries

True Freedom Ministries has the largest prison ministry in the state of Ohio with over 50 volunteers. True Freedom’s prison ministry incorporates a multifaceted approach of evangelism, discipleship, mentorship, and post-release re-entry programming. Last year, over 15,000 inmates attended True Freedom’s prison services. True Freedom also has a Homeless Ministry which has been serving Cleveland’s homeless population for over eighteen years, never missing a Saturday for any reason. It currently serves over 400 hot meals each week. True Freedom ministers through an Addiction Recovery ministry as well. It serves as the centerpiece for True Freedom’s prison re-entry program. The Addiction Recovery program provides for material needs, aftercare and accountability, and case management. Finally, True Freedom has a Nursing Home ministry that ministers to individuals through end of life care.

Fr. Sean currently volunteers in True Freedom Ministries’ Prison and Addiction Recovery ministries.

Alpha

Alpha is a series of sessions exploring the Christian faith, typically run over eleven weeks. Each talk looks at a different question around faith and is designed to create conversation. Alpha is run all around the globe, and everyone is welcome.
