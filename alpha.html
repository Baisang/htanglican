Alpha



Under a constant barrage of troubling news, in the midst of busy schedules, and with the serious challenges of life, it’s easy to wonder, “is there more to life than this?” Alpha is an engaging way to approach the answer to this question..


Alpha explores the Christian faith by sparking conversation about different questions around the topic of faith. A typical Alpha group starts with food—it’s a great way to encourage community and get to know one another. Alpha groups then move to a talk—Alpha talks are an engaging video that explore the big issues around faith and unpack the basics of Christianity. Alpha groups then close with discussion—this is the most important part of Alpha—it’s the chance to share thoughts and ideas on the topic of the talk in a small group. There’s no obligation to say anything and there’s nothing you can’t say. It’s an opportunity to hear from others and contribute your own perspective in an honest, friendly, and open environment.
